Me

Born and raised in Maryland, and yes I enjoy eating crabs + Old Bay. It's what I do.

I completed my BA and MA in mathematics at Johns Hopkins University, worked for 3.5 years at Capital One on asset-backed securitization, and completed my PhD in biostatistics at the University of Washington. I am currently a post-doc at the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center.
