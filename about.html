Me

Born and raised in Maryland, and yes I have seen The Wire. Supposedly there are ways to eat crabs without steaming and seasoning them with Old Bay, but the thought frightens me.

I received a BA and MA in mathematics from Johns Hopkins, worked for 3.5 years at Capital One on asset-backed securitization, and am now working on my PhD in biostatistics at the University of Washington. Seattle has yet to figure out how to properly prepare crabs but is otherwise pretty awesome. In my spare time I try to cling to a roster spot on our incredibly stacked biostat frisbee and softball teams (respectively named the Improper Priors and Residuals).
